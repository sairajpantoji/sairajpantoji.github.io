Their story

Unfolding a fairytale made in heaven.

  • 2009

    Humble Beginnings

    They first met at a bus stop through a common friend. It was very amusing. Hinal's first impression about Dipesh was a flirtatiously outspoken guy. Dipesh thought Hinal as a total introvert nerd. Completely opposite in nature.

  • Feb 2011

    Confessions

    By this time, they had become very good friends. Dipesh realized there is something special between the 2 of them, so he confessed his feelings towards her. He had been special for her also but she was afraid of accepting it. Gradually, he made her realize about her feelings as well and they took their relationship to another level. Yes, it was the best decision of her life!

  • November 2016

    Midnight Dhamaka

    At 4.30am on Hinal's birthday, their door bell rang thrice. She and her parents woke up with a chill. A newspaper was lying outside the door. It was Dipesh's proposal to Hinal's parents for her. After reading that, they went to the window. Dipesh and their entire group were standing below holding led lights that said YES/NO.

  • February 2017

    Officially hooked

    Both of their parents knew they were very good friends since a long time. So, after a series of meeting they decided to make it official to the entire family. February has been so lucky for them!

  • February 2018

    Their big day

    Finally the time has come to make this fairytale a reality. After all the highs and lows and a filmy twist, they are finally getting married on 18th Feb, 2018.
